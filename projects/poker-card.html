Overview

Built a browser-based poker card creation tool that lets users design custom cards with live previews and intuitive controls. The app allows users to input card details such as title, description, stats, and images, and instantly renders a polished card layout in real time. With a focus on clean UI and responsiveness, the project demonstrates front-end development skills, DOM manipulation, and dynamic rendering, while recreating the feel of professional card generators used in games and creative projects.
