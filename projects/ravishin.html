RAVISHIN'

Jun 2025

Python Art Game

Overview

I made this game as a birthday gift for my close friend, which takes place during the end of our senior year of high school, on her birthday. It's a story that includes many people and locations from our lives recreated in a retro format. It's littered with personal and inside jokes (so I won't be sharing a download link to the game, but there are pictures!)

It took months to make and it is one of my favorite projects, combining writing, digital art, and programming!
