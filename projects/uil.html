Calculator Program Collection

Mar 2023

Calculator TI-BASIC Math

Overview

Developed a collection of custom TI-84 calculator programs designed to automate repetitive computations and speed up problem solving in competitive mathematics and UIL Calculator Applications. These tools streamline complex geometry, calculus, algebra, and logarithmic calculations, reducing manual work and improving accuracy under time pressure. The project demonstrates problem-solving ability, mathematical understanding, and practical software development on constrained hardware platforms.

Program Description
FLOW.exp Automates overflow and flow-rate style logarithmic calculations commonly used in UIL Calculator Applications competitions.
CIRCLES.exp Computes properties involving inscribed and circumscribed circles in triangles, including useful geometric relationships.
SOR.exp Solves solids of revolution problems from calculus by automating volume calculations and related formulas.
TRUNCUBE.exp Handles specialized geometry problems involving truncated cubes and corner-cut cube configurations.
SEGSECT.exp Provides quick calculations for segment and sector formulas in circle geometry.
REMAIN.exp Calculates remainders for a broad range of values beyond the limitations of the calculator’s built-in remainder functionality.
PERCENT.exp Automates percent error, percent difference, and related calculations using the exact formulas required in UIL competitions.
FRUSTUM.exp Computes formulas and measurements related to frustums, including truncated cone geometry.
PLANEGEO.exp A collection of plane geometry utilities and formula helpers for competition-style problems.
